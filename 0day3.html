Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our training platform.

Do I need prior experience to get started?
+

No prior experience is required! We offer learning paths for complete beginners through to advanced practitioners. Our introductory courses will help you build foundational knowledge before progressing to more advanced topics.

How does the lab environment work?
+

Our lab environment is entirely cloud-based and accessible through your web browser. You'll get access to virtual machines that simulate real-world networks and systems, allowing you to practice penetration testing and defensive techniques safely.

Are your certifications recognized by employers?
+

Yes! Our certifications are highly regarded in the cybersecurity industry. They focus on practical, hands-on skills that employers value. Many of our certified students have secured positions at leading tech companies and security firms.

What is your pricing model?
+

We offer several subscription options including monthly, quarterly, and annual plans. We also provide special pricing for students and educational institutions. Enterprise plans are available for teams and organizations.
