Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our training platform and courses.

Do I need prior cybersecurity experience?
No, 0day offers learning paths for all levels. Our beginner courses start with cybersecurity fundamentals and gradually progress to advanced topics. We recommend starting with our "Security Foundations" path if you're new to the field.
How does the lab environment work?
Our labs run in cloud-based virtual environments that you access through your browser. Each lab provides a realistic network or application scenario where you can practice techniques without needing your own infrastructure. Labs include guided exercises and challenges.
Are certifications included with courses?
Our certification exams are separate from course materials. However, our courses are specifically designed to prepare you for our certification exams. Each certification path includes practice exams and exam preparation materials.
What kind of support do you offer?
We provide 24/7 lab access support, dedicated mentor assistance for premium plans, active community forums, and regular office hours with security experts. Enterprise plans include dedicated account managers and custom support options.
